The Pig Game is a fun and simple dice game. Two players compete against each other and the first, who reaches 100 points wins. On each turn the player can roll the dice as many times as he wants.

But beware: If you roll a 1, you don't get any points and it's the next player's turn.

The value of the roll is added to a turn total. If you decide to keep your turn total, your turn total is added to your score.

This project was built by following the online course "The Complete JavaScript Course 2021: From Zero to Expert!" by Jonas Schmedtmann. I modified the design a little bit and added the JavaScript parts.
