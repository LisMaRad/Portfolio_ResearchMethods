Reached the 1st place at the "Salzburger Landespreis 2022" in the competition of the University Of Applied Sciences Salzburg (MultiMediaTechnology).

Entinabo is an online friends book. The name is derived from the words "ENTry IN A BOok". The concept of a friend book, as known from elementary school or secondary school, was adapted and brought into an online format.

A book can be created in three steps:

  1. registration
  2. creation of the book
  3. send invitation to friends

To make the entry as easy as possible, the invited friends do not have to register. They will receive an e-mail with a generated link that leads to the form. The form can be filled out once. After that, the link can be used to view the book.

The owner of the book has the possibility to delete, view and export the books/entries as PDF.

This project was created during my MultiMediaTechnology course (2nd semester) at the University of Applied Sciences Salzburg.
